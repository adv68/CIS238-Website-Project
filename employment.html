Work With Us

If you are passionate about health, wellness, and excellent customer service the Smoothie Station is a great place for you. We always have room for enthusiatic individuals looking to expand their horizons. With flexible scheduling, college tuition assistance, and health insurance options for qualified employees, the Smoothie Station is the perfect place to learn and grow.

Below is a list of any current position openings:

Lead Smoothie Artist- Grandville, MI

The Lead Smoothie Artist will be responsible for overseeing daily operations of the Smoothie Station and train and manage the smoothie team. This position requires confidence, dedication, and terrific leadership skills. Your passion for teamwork and nutrition should shine through in your work every day as you work with us to help bring high quality, delicious products to our customers.

Position Responsibilities:

  • Monitor and maintain quality of all products
  • Oversee all aspects of the daily operation of the Smoothie Station
  • Receive, check, and properly store all incoming inventory
  • Know and keep in compliance with health and safety standards
  • Prepare reports as needed and manage scheduling and Station coverage
  • Operate, teach, and supervise safe operation of all applicances and equipment
  • Handle conflict resolution as needed with customers and staff

If you are interested in this position please email the Smoothie Station at SmoothieStation@yahoo.com. Please include the name of the position in your subject line and be sure to include a copy of your resume.
