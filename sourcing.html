What Are GMO's And Why Should I Avoid Them?

GMO stands for: Genetically Modified Organism. Essentially, GMOs are plants whos genetic makeup has been modified. There are a couple of reasons why this is done. Usually it can be to keep the plant safe from viruses, weather conditions, or even make them immune to harsh pesticieds. The down side to this is GMO's can be seen as unsafe and have been known to cause:

  • Increase in allergies
  • Increase enviromental damage
  • Cancer
