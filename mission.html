Our Mission

We are a health concious and environmentally friendly smoothie bar that focuses on serving smoothies that are good for your body and the environment. We work to help our customers listen to their bodies' needs and fuel themselves in a mindful way that encompasses not just good flavor but also nutritionally dense ingredients that provide healthy, long-lasting energy to get them through their day.

We want to be the place where our community can turn for consistent high quality products that they know are sustainably sourced and good for them. Smoothie Station was created by two nutritionist and registered dietitians with a passion for health and wellness. Each smoothie was carefully created to be nutritionally varied and absolutely delicious!

We build our smoothies with all-natural, organic products including vegetables, fruits, whole grains, legumes, nuts, and seeds. We also offer the ability to personalize your smoothie with plant-based milks, gluten free options, and added shots of wheat grass. We work with our customers to provide what they need to fuel their bodies, keeping in mind that each person's preferences and needs are unique.

Our goal at the Smoothie Station is to help lead our community to a healthier place by fueling their bodies with whole real food and avoiding preservatives and additives. We want to help our customers head down a road to better health and promise to always do our part to provide only the healthiest and freshest products to them.

We appreciate your support and look forward to working with you to help you become the healthiest version of you.
