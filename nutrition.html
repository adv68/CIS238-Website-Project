Eat Fresh With Smoothie Station!

We cater to a variety of diets and are an allergen friendly facility! If you have any questions contact any of our 20 US locations during our regular business hours (8am-8pm EST).

Gluten Free Options

All menu items can be made gluten free. If you have a gluten allergy ask one of our employees to use the allergen friendly equipment.

If an item is already gluten free it will be listed on our menu with a (G) beside the item name

Vegan and Vegetarian Options

Any non-vegan or vegetarian ingredients will be listed alongside menu items for transparency. Here at Smoothie Station we are committed to serving our customers and meeting all dietary needs. In addition vegan items are marked with a (V) and vegetarian items are marked with a (VG).

For questions about our ethical sourcing and distribution ask any of our 20 US locations for further details. All products used in our smoothies are cruelty free

Allergens

If you have any food alleries to our menu items let one of our employees know and they will go through our allergen procedure. We take allerens very seriously here at Smoothie Station and make sure new gloves are used and have separate allergen friendly machines and workspaces.

Allergens used at our locations:

  • dairy
  • gluten
  • soy

Although we have a strong commitment to catering to customers with food allergies there is always a risk of cross-contamination and suggest that those with severe food allergies order Smoothie Station menu items at their own risk.

Our Commitment to all Organic!

At Smoothie Station we care about what we are serving in our products which is why we are committed all organic!

We believe that in addition to the health benefits to organic produce, it also enhances our smoothies taste! Eating fresh, locally sourced fresh fruit will help give you the energy you need to get out and take on the day and fuel you for your next adventure.
